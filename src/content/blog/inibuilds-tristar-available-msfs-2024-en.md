The wait is over. Announced in December 2025 and detailed in February with system deep-dives that built serious anticipation, the **Lockheed L-1011-500 TriStar** by **iniBuilds** officially launched on March 31, 2026 on the **MSFS 2024 Marketplace** for **$49.99 USD**. Lockheed's legendary trijet — the third widebody in aviation history after the Boeing 747 and DC-10 — finally joins the Microsoft simulator fleet with a level of fidelity that honors its reputation as an aircraft ahead of its time. For those who read [our announcement article](/blog/inibuilds-tristar-l1011-msfs-2024/), here is what the final release delivers.

![iniBuilds TriStar available on the MSFS 2024 Marketplace](/images/tristar/marketplace.webp)

## A trijet with five seats

The L-1011 cockpit is a 1970s time capsule — and that is precisely what makes it fascinating. The crew consisted of three members: captain, first officer, and **flight engineer**, the latter managing a dedicated panel covering electrical, engine, fuel, pneumatic, pressurization, and hydraulic systems. iniBuilds modeled all five cockpit seats and the complete flight engineer panel.

For pilots flying solo, a **virtual flight engineer** handles the third crew member's duties: systems management, procedures, and callouts. It is an elegant solution to the inherent challenge of simulating an aircraft designed for three — you can focus on flying while the virtual flight engineer manages the rest, or disable the assistance and handle everything yourself.

![L-1011 TriStar cockpit — flight engineer panel](/images/tristar/flight-engineer.webp)

## Navigation: from INS to GPS

The iniBuilds TriStar offers three navigation modes via the **Paper Flight Bag** system:

- **Radnav** — Classic VOR/NDB navigation, faithfully reproduced
- **INS** — The triple inertial navigation system, the centerpiece of the real aircraft, with full alignment and waypoint entry
- **GPS** — An optional Garmin mounted on the glareshield, preserving the vintage cockpit aesthetic while providing modern precision

The **SimBrief** integration is particularly well executed: a map screen has been repurposed as a batch INS waypoint import tool with route visualization. A detail that shows the care given to usability without compromising period authenticity.

![TriStar Paper Flight Bag — configuration and navigation management](/images/tristar/paper-flight-bag.webp)

## CAT IIIB autoland and AFCS

The TriStar was ahead of its time: it was one of the first airliners certified for **CAT IIIA automatic landing** from its entry into service in 1972. The iniBuilds **Automatic Flight Control System** (AFCS) replicates this capability with realistic autopilot behavior, authentic flare logic, derotation behavior, and post-landing rollout control. The autoland extends to **CAT IIIB** — a performance level that few add-ons reproduce with this fidelity.

![TriStar AFCS system — autopilot and autoland](/images/tristar/afcs.webp)

## Echo19 sound: recorded on a real L-1011

The sound environment deserves its own chapter. **Echo19 Audio** captured samples directly from an operational L-1011 at the **TriStar Experience** in Kansas City — one of the last accessible examples in the world. The result covers RB211 engine behavior at every thrust setting, cockpit ambience with period-correct background sounds, and even cabin airflow. This is not a synthetic soundpack: it is the real sound of the TriStar, brought into MSFS.

## Cabin and exterior

The cabin reproduces the L-1011's distinctive features: the characteristic **vaulted ceiling**, single-side overhead bins, the **retractable airstairs** built into the fuselage, and — a delightful detail — the **circular lavatory carousel** positioned directly below the center engine S-duct. Exterior textures include operational wear: scratches, stains, and patina that give the aircraft a believable lived-in appearance.

![L-1011 TriStar in Eastern Air Lines livery at the gate — MSFS 2024](/images/tristar/cabin-release.webp)

## 12+ historic liveries

The launch package includes liveries from iconic TriStar operators:

- **British Airways**, **TWA**, **Delta**, **Pan Am**, **United** — the major American and British carriers
- **Cathay Pacific**, **Saudia**, **Royal Jordanian** — Asia and the Middle East
- **LTU**, **Air Transat**, **Novair** — charter operators
- Additional variants are available via **iniManager** on PC

Each livery features PBR textures with dynamic lighting response, enhancing realism at any time of day.

![TriStar cockpit dynamic lighting](/images/tristar/lighting.webp)

## 150-page manual and 15% off airports

iniBuilds does not just deliver an aircraft: the package includes a **150-page manual**, a dedicated FAQ covering INS workflows, the Paper Flight Bag, and flight engineer functions, plus recommended route planning via **FlightSim Dispatch** — a database of historic L-1011 routes.

To celebrate the launch, a **15% discount** is available on airports historically linked to the TriStar: **Dubai, Heathrow, Gatwick, and Honolulu**.
