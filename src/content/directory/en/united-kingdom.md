The United Kingdom is one of the most important countries in the flight simulation world. Home to pioneering addon developers, a strong network of virtual airlines, and a vibrant content creator scene, the UK punches well above its weight in the global flight sim community. British developers have shaped the hobby for decades, from early FSX-era publishers to modern studios building study-level airliners for MSFS and X-Plane.

The UK's position is underpinned by a thriving aviation heritage and some of the busiest airspace in Europe. Both VATSIM and IVAO maintain active UK divisions, providing virtual ATC coverage across the country's complex terminal areas and en-route sectors. British virtual airlines representing carriers from British Airways to easyJet attract thousands of pilots, while community groups keep VFR flying and social events alive.

On the development side, the UK is home to iniBuilds, Just Flight, DC Designs, and several specialist scenery studios, making it one of the most productive countries for flight simulation add-ons. A strong roster of YouTube creators, many of them real-world professional pilots, rounds out the ecosystem with tutorials, reviews, and live streams.

## ATC Networks

**VATSIM UK** is the United Kingdom Division of the VATSIM network, providing virtual air traffic control across the entire UK Flight Information Region. The division operates a structured ATC training programme and a Flying Programme that pairs student pilots with experienced mentors for one-on-one instruction.

**IVAO United Kingdom and Ireland** covers UK and Irish airspace on the IVAO network, with over 5,000 members and more than one million combined online hours. The division runs training aerodromes at Stansted, Edinburgh, Luton, and Shannon, and hosts regular events and cross-divisional flights.

## Virtual Airlines

**BAVirtual** is the UK's premier British Airways virtual airline and one of the oldest in the hobby, operating continuously since April 2000. With over 1,000 registered pilots and 2 million logged flight hours, it offers VATSIM-accredited shared cockpit training, realistic British Airways documentation, and a structured pilot career system.

**Fly UK Virtual Airways** has been operating for over 20 years as a leading UK-based international virtual airline. It serves 200+ worldwide destinations with its live SkyTrack flight tracking system and also runs a Flying Club for general aviation enthusiasts, supporting MSFS, X-Plane, and Prepar3D.

**JetVA**, established in 2010, is the original easyJet virtual airline and now also simulates Jet2 operations. It uses a custom BlueSky tracking system with SimBrief integration and VATSIM compatibility. **JetStream Virtual** takes a different approach as a multi-airline platform, hosting 146 real-world carriers — including British Airways, easyJet, Ryanair, Jet2, TUI Airways, and Virgin Atlantic — with schedules refreshed daily from real-world data.

## Communities

**The Pilot Club** is a welcoming aviation and flight sim community and official VATSIM partner. It offers scheduled group flights, a free training programme, the TPC Charters virtual airline, and an active Discord server with over 6,500 members. Partner discounts on flight sim products are available to members.

**CIX VFR Club** is a long-running UK-based club with around 800 members, dedicated to VFR light aircraft flying. The club runs group flights five evenings a week on VATSIM with TeamSpeak voice communications, along with monthly themed events and training courses for pilots of all experience levels.

## Addon Developers

**iniBuilds**, headquartered in London, has become one of the most prominent flight simulation studios in the world. Their high-fidelity Airbus lineup for MSFS and X-Plane — spanning the A300, A310, A320, A340, A350, and A380 — is complemented by detailed airport scenery and a growing storefront that hosts third-party developers.

**Just Flight**, based in Huntingdon, Cambridgeshire, is one of the world's largest publishers and developers of flight simulation add-ons, active since 1995. Their extensive catalogue spans aircraft, scenery, and AI traffic packages for MSFS, X-Plane, and Prepar3D, and they also distribute products from independent developers worldwide.

**DC Designs**, founded in 2017 by Dean Crawford in Kent, specialises in military and iconic aircraft for MSFS. Their award-winning catalogue includes the F-14 Tomcat, F-15 Eagle, Concorde, AV-8B Harrier II, and F-4 Phantom II.

The UK also has a strong scenery development scene. **UK2000 Scenery** is a veteran developer producing FPS-friendly, photographic HD airport add-ons for MSFS and Prepar3D. **Pilot Plus** creates detailed airport environments for MSFS, X-Plane, and Prepar3D, with a portfolio covering London Biggin Hill, Bristol International, and London Southend. **Burning Blue Design** focuses on detailed small airfields and regional airports across the UK for MSFS.

## Training

**Deeside Flight Simulators**, based in Cheshire, operates a dedicated simulator centre with Boeing 737, Airbus A320, helicopter, and PA28 Warrior flightdecks. The centre offers pilot training sessions, pre-assessment preparation for real-world flight tests, and gift experience packages.

## Media and Content Creators

The UK boasts one of the strongest collections of flight simulation YouTube creators in the world, with several channels run by real-world professional pilots.

**320 Sim Pilot**, a real-world Boeing 777 pilot, has over 100,000 subscribers and produces detailed tutorials, aircraft reviews, and live streams covering both MSFS and X-Plane, with a particular focus on Airbus and Boeing procedures.

**SimHanger Flight Simulation** (76K subscribers) covers MSFS with how-to guides, hardware reviews, head tracking and VR comparisons, scenery showcases, and regular multiplayer group flights. **Into the Blue Simulations** (66K subscribers) is another real-world professional pilot producing cinematic aircraft reviews, full flight videos, and tutorials across MSFS and X-Plane.

**Drawyah** (39K subscribers) produces MSFS tutorials, live flights, aircraft reviews, and first-look videos, and is also active on Twitch. **FilbertFlies** (21K subscribers), active since the 1990s, is known for in-depth tutorials featuring real-world pilots, add-on reviews, and VATSIM group flights.

## Hardware

**Flightstore**, established in 1999, is a UK pilot supply shop carrying over 10,000 products. Their flight simulation section includes yokes, rudder pedals, throttle quadrants, and joysticks from major manufacturers, alongside real-world aviation equipment.
